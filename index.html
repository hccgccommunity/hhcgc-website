About

Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to celebrating Hindu culture, faith, and values while serving the cultural, educational, social, and spiritual needs of the community. Guided by inclusivity and the belief in seeing divinity in all, it brings together people of all generations, from children to seniors, in a shared space of learning, connection, and service. Rooted in the principles of Samaj (community), Sahyog (collaboration), Sanskruti (culture), Shikshan (education), and Sewa (service), the organization supports meaningful programs, cultural celebrations, and educational initiatives that help families feel connected and engaged. It plays a vital role in strengthening community bonds, preserving Hindu heritage, and building a positive future for the next generation, while serving as a center for unity, belonging, and growth in Greater Cincinnati.

Vision

To build a vibrant, enduring Hindu community center where families grow in identity, belonging, service, and cultural confidence.

Mission

To provide space for spiritual learning, youth development, cultural celebrations, educational programming, and meaningful community connection.

Core Values

We are guided by Dharma, seva, integrity, service, family unity, respect, cultural continuity, and responsible leadership.

Facility Rental

Our spaces are designed to bring people together whether for cultural programs, classes, celebrations, or community gatherings. From small group sessions to larger events, each room offers flexibility and comfort to support a variety of needs, including group seating, presentations, and multi-purpose use. A minimum booking of 2 hours is required, with discounted pricing available for weekday bookings and extended reservations of 4 hours or more. We strive to create a welcoming environment where families, friends, and community members can connect, learn, and celebrate together.

Surya Hall

Ideal for large gatherings, workshops, classes, and community events during weekdays.
• Room Size: 30 × 40 ft (1200 sq ft)
• Capacity: Up to 120 guests

Samskriti Hall

Ideal for larger gatherings, workshops, cultural events, and group activities during weekdays.
• Room Size: 18 × 47 ft (836 sq ft)
• Capacity: Up to 100 guests

Nataraj Room

Ideal for dance classes, rehearsals, workshops, and group sessions during weekdays.
• Room Size: 19 × 34 ft (600 sq ft)
• Capacity: Up to 40 guests

Ganesh Room

Ideal for meetings and small gatherings during weekdays.
• Room Size: 13 × 18 ft (250 sq ft)
• Capacity: Up to 12 guests
