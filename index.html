About HHGCC

A community-centered website with a spiritual and cultural heartbeat.

This site is structured to present HHGCC as a trusted nonprofit destination for families, community leaders, youth, and donors. It combines a warm, values-based visual identity with practical functionality for room rentals, leadership visibility, community trust, and outreach.

What this website is designed to do

Present HHGCC’s story, explain its purpose, and make it easy for people to understand how the center serves children, families, educational activities, spiritual gatherings, volunteer programs, cultural programs, and community celebrations.

The structure also supports hall rentals through a clear five-room booking system, terms and conditions, donation prompts, trustee and board profiles, and space for your banners, logo, rounded headshots, and other media.

Room Booking

Reserve a hall or room with a simple hourly booking flow.

This static-site version lets visitors review all five spaces, choose a room, enter their requested date and time, agree to the terms, and continue to Calendly for the final booking and payment step.

Booking terms and conditions

  • Reservations are subject to availability and are confirmed only after completion of the Calendly flow and payment.
  • Hourly booking windows, setup time, and cleanup time must be included in the requested reservation period.
  • The renter is responsible for room condition, permitted usage, and compliance with HHGCC rules and community standards.
  • Decorations, catering, AV usage, footwear rules, and cleanup expectations can be customized once your final policy is provided.
  • HHGCC reserves the right to deny or cancel bookings that do not align with facility use policies or nonprofit community guidelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for visitors, renters, and donors.

Can I reserve a room for just one hour?

Yes. This site is structured for hourly bookings. Final minimums, setup windows, and cleanup requirements can be adjusted in the policy section.

How does payment work?

The static site sends visitors to a room-specific Calendly link. Your payment link can sit behind that booking flow so no custom checkout system is required on the website itself.

Can this site include our trustees, members, and advisors?

Yes. The layout is already prepared for detailed leadership sections with circular photos, titles, and bios.

Can we add events, gallery, or volunteer forms later?

Yes. Since this is a static site, it can easily grow into a fuller nonprofit community website with additional pages or sections.
