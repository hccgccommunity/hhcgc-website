Khel Sandhya Terms & Conditions

Please review the terms and conditions before participating in Khel Sandhya.

By participating in Khel Sandhya at HCCGC, all participants agree to follow the rules and conditions listed below.

Eligibility

Only individuals aged 12 years and above may participate in Khel Sandhya unless HCCGC explicitly states otherwise for a specific activity or session.

Respectful Conduct

All participants are expected to behave respectfully toward fellow participants, volunteers, staff, and the HCCGC facility. Harassment, abusive language, intimidation, aggressive conduct, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

Use of HCCGC Games Only

Participants may play only those games that are arranged or approved by HCCGC for Khel Sandhya. Bringing games, gaming equipment, or related materials from outside is not permitted unless expressly approved in advance by HCCGC.

No Betting or Gambling

No betting, wagering, gambling, prize pooling based on monetary stakes, or any gambling-style version of the games is allowed. All games must remain recreational, friendly, and non-commercial in nature.

Smoking, Alcohol, and Prohibited Substances

Smoking, vaping, alcohol consumption, illegal substances, intoxicating materials, and non-vegetarian food are strictly prohibited anywhere on the premises during Khel Sandhya.

Care of the Property

Participants must respect the facility, furniture, equipment, décor, and all other property of HCCGC. Damaging, misusing, or removing any item from the premises is strictly prohibited.

Cleanliness Requirement

The venue must be kept reasonably clean during and after the event. All trash must be disposed of properly, and food or drinks should not be left on tables, floors, or outdoor areas.

Safety and Facility Decorum

Participants must follow all safety instructions and maintain the decorum of the facility. Running, rough behavior, misuse of furniture, blocking walkways, or any unsafe activity is not allowed.

Noise and Community Respect

Participants must maintain reasonable noise levels and respect neighboring properties. Excessive noise or disruptive behavior may result in the activity being stopped and the participant being asked to leave.

Parking Regulations

Vehicles must be parked only in designated parking areas. Blocking entrances, driveways, or emergency exits is strictly prohibited.

Personal Belongings

Participants are responsible for their own belongings. HCCGC is not responsible for any lost, stolen, or damaged personal items brought to the event.

Injury and Liability

Participation in Khel Sandhya is voluntary. Each participant assumes responsibility for their own participation and any associated risks. HCCGC, its organizers, volunteers, and representatives shall not be liable for accidental injury, loss, damage, or claims arising from participation, except as required by applicable law.

Photo and Video Consent

HCCGC may take photographs or videos during Khel Sandhya for community updates, website use, or promotional purposes. By participating, attendees consent to such use unless they specifically inform HCCGC otherwise in advance.

Right to Refuse or Remove Participation

HCCGC reserves the right to deny entry or ask any participant to leave the premises if these rules are violated, if conduct is disruptive or unsafe, or if the activity is deemed inconsistent with the values and rules of the event or facility.
