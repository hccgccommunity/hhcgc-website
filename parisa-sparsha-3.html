About the Concert

A Celebration of Timeless Melodies

An intimate, curated evening that brings legendary film music to life through the eyes and ears of a master composer.

HCCGC proudly presents Parisa Sparsh — an intimate musical mahfil with renowned vocalist, violinist, composer, and music arranger Sachin Ingale from Pune, India. Sachin ji is an All India Radio approved singer and an 'A' grade music composer with a distinguished career spanning Marathi plays, daily soaps, jingles, feature films, and documentaries.

He has performed as a singer, violinist, harmonium player, and music arranger in celebrated programs such as Soor Taal, Nakshatranche Dene, Jeevan Gane, and Saam Gurukul.

Parisa Sparsh explores the thoughts, emotions, and inspirations behind timeless compositions. Through the lens of a composer, Sachin ji takes the audience on a beautiful journey through the works of legendary Marathi and Hindi film music composers, blended seamlessly with his own original compositions — a truly unique musical experience you would not want to miss.

While the program features both Marathi and Hindi songs, the narration throughout the evening will be in Marathi.

Seating is on a first come, first served basis. We encourage you to arrive early to find a comfortable spot.

What to Expect

  • A composer's insight into the minds of legendary Marathi and Hindi film music composers
  • Original compositions by Sachin Ingale blended seamlessly with classic works
  • Live violin, vocals, harmonium, tabla, and percussion
  • Narration in Marathi throughout the evening
  • An intimate mahfil-style community concert setting

Who Should Attend

  • Lovers of Marathi and Hindi film music traditions
  • Those who understand or appreciate the Marathi language
  • Families and community members of all ages
  • Anyone who wishes to support HCCGC
