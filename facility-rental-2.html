Guidelines

What You Need to Know

To ensure a welcoming experience for everyone, please review our rental policies before submitting your booking.

Discounts & Pricing

  • Weekday discount: 20% off Mon–Fri bookings
  • Extended booking: additional 20% off for 4+ hours
  • Nonprofit rate: additional 25% off for registered nonprofits
  • Discounts stack — multiple savings can apply to a single booking
  • Contact us via email at hccgccommunity@gmail.com for custom nonprofit pricing

Booking & Setup

  • Bookings are confirmed via email within 2–3 business days
  • Setup and breakdown time must be included in booked hours
  • Renters are responsible for leaving the space clean and in its original condition. Please review the Terms & Conditions
  • All renters agree to HCCGC's community standards of respect and inclusivity
  • For questions, reach out via email at hccgccommunity@gmail.com

💬 You can also reach us and stay updated via the HCCGC Facility Rental group in our WhatsApp community.
