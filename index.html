Room Booking

Reserve one of five rooms on an hourly basis.

Visitors can review room capacity and usage, select a room, enter date and time, accept the terms, and continue to the correct Calendly link.

Terms and conditions

  • Reservations are subject to availability and are only confirmed after Calendly selection and payment completion.
  • Requested booking time must include setup and cleanup.
  • The renter is responsible for room condition, community conduct, and compliance with HHGCC usage rules.
  • Decoration limits, food policy, footwear rules, AV usage, and cleanup requirements can be customized later.
  • HHGCC may deny or cancel bookings that do not align with facility policy or nonprofit community standards.
