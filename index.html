About

Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati is a nonprofit organization dedicated to celebrating Hindu culture, faith, and values while serving the cultural, educational, social, and spiritual needs of the community. Guided by inclusivity and the belief in seeing divinity in all, it brings together people of all generations, from children to seniors, in a shared space of learning, connection, and service. Rooted in the principles of Samaj (community), Sahyog (collaboration), Sanskruti (culture), Shikshan (education), and Sewa (service), the organization supports meaningful programs, cultural celebrations, and educational initiatives that help families feel connected and engaged. It plays a vital role in strengthening community bonds, preserving Hindu heritage, and building a positive future for the next generation, while serving as a center for unity, belonging, and growth in Greater Cincinnati.

Vision

A vibrant space where Hindu Americans come together to celebrate, learn, and share their culture, faith, and values - while contributing to society at large rooted in inclusivity and the belief that divinity exists in everyone.

Mission

Offer programs and services that nurture the cultural, educational, social, and spiritual needs of the Hindu community and the wider society - fostering pluralism, unity, and a deep commitment to service.

Core Values

We are guided by Dharma, Sewa, Integrity, Service, Family Unity, Respect, Cultural Continuity, and Responsible Leadership.

Initiatives

Our initiatives focus on creating meaningful opportunities for connection, learning, and engagement within the community. From dedicated gatherings that foster companionship and well-being for seniors to enriching programs that encourage curiosity and cultural learning in children, each initiative is thoughtfully designed to support different stages of life.

These programs create spaces where individuals can connect, participate, and grow, while reinforcing shared traditions and values in ways that feel relevant and accessible for today’s generation.

Sahyog Milan - A Gathering for Seniors

We want to create a joyful space where Hindu seniors feel valued and supported. Together we’ll share meals, play games, enjoy conversations, can celebrate culture while inspiring the next generation and society at large with our rich experience.


Aum School - Summer Camp

Aum School Summer Camp is a culturally enriching program that is curated to nurture young minds through Hindu values and engaging, age-appropriate learning. It blends stories, shlokas, games, and creative activities to help children build character, confidence, and a strong connection to their roots. The program focuses on holistic development while making cultural learning fun and meaningful.

Facility Rental

Our spaces are designed to bring people together whether for cultural programs, classes, celebrations, or community gatherings. From small group sessions to larger events, each room offers flexibility and comfort to support a variety of needs, including group seating, presentations, and multi-purpose use. A minimum booking of 2 hours is required, with discounted pricing available for weekday bookings and extended reservations of 4 hours or more. We strive to create a welcoming environment where families, friends, and community members can connect, learn, and celebrate together.
