Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati (HCCGC)

Celebrating Hindu culture, faith, and values. Our goal is to serve the cultural, educational, social, and spiritual needs of our community.

We are guided by inclusivity and seeing divinity in all. Our programs unite Hindus of all generations from children to seniors.

  • HCCGC is dedicated to supporting the Hindu community through culture, education, service, and shared values.
  • HCCGC creates a place where families, children, youth, adults, and seniors can all feel connected and supported.
  • HCCGC helps strengthen community bonds by offering meaningful programs, celebrations, and learning opportunities.
  • HCCGC works to preserve Hindu heritage while building a positive future for the next generation.
  • HCCGC stands as a center for unity, belonging, and community growth in Greater Cincinnati.

Vision

To build a vibrant, enduring Hindu community center where families grow in identity, belonging, service, and cultural confidence.

Mission

To provide space for spiritual learning, youth development, cultural celebrations, educational programming, and meaningful community connection.

Core Values

Dharma, seva, integrity, volunteerism, family unity, respect for elders, cultural continuity, and responsible leadership.
