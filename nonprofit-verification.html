Relationship Between HCCGC and hccgc.org

Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati (HCCGC) is the public-facing name of Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati Corporation, an Ohio nonprofit organization recognized as tax-exempt under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

The domain hccgc.org is owned, operated, and maintained by Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati Corporation and serves as the organization's official website.

All official communications, community programs, educational initiatives, cultural events, facility information, volunteer opportunities, and charitable activities of the organization are conducted through and promoted on hccgc.org.

Our Mission

The Hindu Community Center of Greater Cincinnati (HCCGC) exists to foster cultural, educational, spiritual, and community connections for individuals and families in the Greater Cincinnati region.

Through community-centered programs and initiatives, HCCGC seeks to preserve and share Hindu values, Indian heritage, cultural traditions, wellness practices, educational opportunities, and opportunities for community service and engagement.
